How to play ?

DemocraticChess is a game about compromises and teamwork. It is a simple game of chess, with all normal rules, except that you can play up to 128 players by each team!

As soon as your team get to move, you have 40 seconds to vote for what you think is to do next. Move a rook, castle your king or rekt that stupid pawn en-passant style, everything is good. When everyone is done voting, or when the clock runs out, the move with the most vote is cast.

If there's a tie in the votes, the move where the voters have the most point is cast. To win point, you must be in the first ones to cast your vote on a move that gets chosen thereafter.

If all players from a team quits or do not vote, there is a win by abandon. If a player do not vote three times in a row, he is kicked out of the game.

After joining the game, you will be automatically added to one team. You will be able to play at their next move.
